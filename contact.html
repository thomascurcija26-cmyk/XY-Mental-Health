Our Location

XY Mental Health is proudly based in Toronto, Ontario, serving individuals across the city and surrounding communities. Our space is designed to feel calm, private, and welcoming—giving you an environment where you can speak openly and focus on your well-being without distractions.

Located in one of Canada’s most diverse and fast-paced cities, we understand the unique pressures that come with urban life, from career demands to social expectations. Our goal is to provide a consistent, reliable space where you can step away from that pressure and work on your mental health with clarity and support.

Stay Connected

Staying connected is an important part of maintaining your mental well-being, and at XY Mental Health, we’re here to support you beyond your sessions. Whether you’re looking for helpful resources, updates, or simple reminders to take care of yourself, we offer ways to stay engaged and informed.

Follow us on our social platforms for mental health tips, insights, and real conversations that break down stigma and keep things relatable. You can also subscribe to our newsletter to receive practical advice, updates on workshops and groups, and tools you can apply in your everyday life.

If you ever have questions, need guidance, or are ready to take the next step, don’t hesitate to reach out. We’re here to support you—whenever you need it.
